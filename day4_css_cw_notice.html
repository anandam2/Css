All students are hereby informed that the Half-Yearly Examinations will begin from Monday, 14th October 2025. The detailed timetable and seating arrangement will be displayed on the school notice board as well as shared by the class teachers.

All are advised to use their time wisely for revision and preparation. We wish all students the very best of luck for their upcoming exams.
